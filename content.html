Trust in Allah

Qur'anic verses to strengthen your faith and provide comfort in difficult times

Relief After Hardship 94:5-6
فَإِنَّ مَعَ الْعُسْرِ يُسْرًا ۝ إِنَّ مَعَ الْعُسْرِ يُسْرًا
"So, surely with hardship comes ease. Indeed, with hardship comes ease."
Allah repeats this promise twice for emphasis. Hardship is always paired with ease — not after, but during. This is Allah's mercy and a reminder to never lose hope, no matter how difficult the situation seems.

Divine Decree 9:51
قُل لَّن يُصِيبَنَا إِلَّا مَا كَتَبَ ٱللَّهُ لَنَا ۚ هُوَ مَوْلَىٰنَا ۚ وَعَلَى ٱللَّهِ فَلْيَتَوَكَّلِ ٱلْمُؤْمِنُونَ
"Nothing will ever befall us except what Allah has decreed for us; He is our Protector."
Every trial you face has been carefully measured and ordained by Allah. This verse reminds us that our challenges are not random, but come from the One who loves us most and knows what's best for our spiritual growth.

Within Your Capacity 2:286
لَا يُكَلِّفُ ٱللَّهُ نَفْسًا إِلَّا وُسْعَهَا
"Allah does not burden a soul beyond what it can bear."
If you're facing a challenge, know that Allah has already equipped you with the strength to overcome it. This verse is a powerful reminder that you are stronger than you think, and Allah will never test you beyond your capacity.
Share
Divine Sufficiency 65:3
وَمَن يَتَوَكَّلْ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ فَهُوَ حَسْبُهُ
"And whoever puts his trust in Allah – He is sufficient for him."
True reliance on Allah means understanding that you don't need to have all the answers or solutions. When you sincerely trust in Allah, He becomes your sustainer, protector, and the fulfiller of all your needs in ways you couldn't imagine.
Share
Strength in Faith 3:139
وَلَا تَهِنُوا وَلَا تَحْزَنُوا وَأَنتُمُ ٱلْأَعْلَوْنَ إِن كُنتُم مُّؤْمِنِينَ
"So do not weaken and do not grieve, and you will be superior if you are [true] believers."
This verse connects faith with strength. As believers, we have access to divine help that makes us ultimately victorious, even if the current situation appears difficult. The command to avoid weakness and grief is followed by the promise of superiority through faith.
Share
Divine Assistance 8:10
وَمَا جَعَلَهُ ٱللَّهُ إِلَّا بُشْرَىٰ وَلِتَطْمَئِنَّ بِهِۦ قُلُوبُكُمْ ۚ وَمَا ٱلنَّصْرُ إِلَّا مِنْ عِندِ ٱللَّهِ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ عَزِيزٌ حَكِيمٌ
"Allah only made it a message of hope and assurance to your hearts. Victory comes only from Allah. Surely Allah is Almighty, All-Wise."
This verse reminds us that divine help often comes in unexpected ways. Allah provides signs and assurances to comfort our hearts before the actual relief comes. True victory is from Allah alone, and it arrives in the perfect form and time according to His wisdom.
